Mission

The mission of VentureR is to be an all-encompassing tool for investing in Virtual Reality related projects and companies owned by entrepreneurs with limited resources. Not only can investors provide capital, they can provide investment in the form of mentoring and business training. Entrepreneurs will be able to list their projects Entrepreneurs will be able to receive funding donated by investors for said projects through the e-commerce toolchain Stripe. Entrepreneurs are able to approach investors that are offering mentoring and training opportunities and learn from industry experienced individuals.
